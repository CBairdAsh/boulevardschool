Where the adventure unfolds

Our Kindergarten program focuses on cultivating your child’s curiosity and creativity. Because every child is unique, our curriculum is designed to move at your child’s own pace, following their learning style and natural interests.

For every child however, we off a blend of social skills activities and academic learning:

- Math for critical thinking & problem solving
- Phonics for reading and writing
- Art to inspire creative self-expression
- Movement to encourage health & well-being

By the end of our school year, all our students will have the skills and confidence they’ll need for a positive elementary school experience.

Where the adventure unfolds

Green space is hard to come by in LA, but we’ve got plenty. Our campus is the perfect environment for little explorers to stretch their minds and bodies, with 1 whole acre of dedicated outdoor space.

In addition to our regular curriculum, we also offer:

- Swimming Lessons (in our heated pool)
- Martial Arts
- Yoga
- Dance
- Fitness
- Gymnastics
- Story Arts
- Judaica

A Happy Place Since 1974

When my parents established The Boulevard School in 1974, they had a vision for early childhood education that was rooted in one word: Love.
‍
They wanted to create an educational environment where children felt nurtured and nourished, safe and supported, and inspired to explore all aspects of growth and development with a sense of adventure.
‍
The Hebrew phrase, Tikkun Olam, which roughly means, “repairing the world” captures the essence of their core belief: that in preparing children to be confident, capable, caring human beings we will contribute to a better world.
‍
I believe this mission to cultivate an ecosystem of love is the reason we’re still here 50 years later, with many second-generation families who drive by and say, “That’s my school!”

Our school is an extension of your home, and a place where students, siblings, parents, teachers and administrators are like family. We look forward to welcoming you into the fold.

Your Partner In Success,

Ms. Rodi, M.S. Ed; M.S.W., Head Administrator

Our Guiding Values

With more than 50 years in business, we’ve seen it all. The fads, the fails, and the bright futures written on little faces. We use these guiding values to stay grounded in the ever-changing environment of education

Be Curious
We’re committed to inspiring a life-long love of learning, which will support your child wherever they go
Share
Everyone’s health and well-being depends on being in community with others as we journey through life and learning
Clean Up Messes
All journeys have their bumps. We believe in navigating them with integrity, courage, and kindness
